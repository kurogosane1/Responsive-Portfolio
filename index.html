About Me

I have a bachelors in Business Administration from University of Houston - Bauer College of Business where I was focused my attention towards Accounting. I always was interested in programing but always assumed that you needed to have a Bachelors degree and needed to be a mad scientist in math to program.

As I got into different roles in Accounting I started noticing a trend where computer programs in the cloud were replacing the traditional installed operating programs. I moved to an oil and gas company where I was surprised to discover that the Accountants where at times required to create a Macro using excel. I never heard of Macro's until I saw someone creating a macro in Excel using programming codes.

It sparked my interest and was tempted to learn programming but wanted help to learn programming. I came across one ad of UT Austin Bootcamp and decided then there itself that I would join the program and here is the result that you see.
